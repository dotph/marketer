## AWARENESS ADS (Weeks 1–3)

> **Rule (cold ad sets):** No product mention. No dotPH branding. Pure problem education.
> **Rule (warm ad set):** Lead with the problem, but include a soft product teaser (e.g., "dotPH is launching free verification para sa businesses"). See [verified-marketing-strategy.md](verified-marketing-strategy.md) for warm awareness ad copy variants (#13–#15).
> **Objective:** Engagement / Video Views — build retargeting audiences.

### Ad 1: "423% Surge" — Stat-Driven Hook

**Stage:** Awareness
**Based on:** Reference #2 (Check Point Philippine Threat Landscape Report 2025)
**Target:** All business owners PH, 25–55

**Primary Text:**
Phishing websites impersonating Philippine businesses jumped 423% in one year.

From 731 to 3,824.

Fake brand profiles on social media? Up 37%.

Scammers aren't slowing down. They're scaling up — and they don't need to hack anything. They just copy your name, your logo, your photos. Tapos hihintayin lang nila na magbayad 'yung customers mo.

The question isn't whether it's happening. The question is — nangyayari na ba sa'yo?

**Headline:** Phishing Sites Up 423% in PH
**Description:** Is your business being copied?

**Creative direction:** Static image or motion graphic. Dark background, bold white "423%" center-screen. Smaller text below: "increase in phishing websites impersonating PH businesses." Source citation in bottom corner: "Check Point, 2025." No logo, no CTA button — engagement objective only.
