# Performance Benchmarks Reference

> Use these as orientation and calibration, not hard rules. Benchmarks vary by industry, offer, audience temperature, and seasonality. Always compare your campaigns against your own historical baseline first.

### Frequency Benchmarks by Funnel Stage

| Audience | Acceptable Frequency | Fatigue Signal | Kill Signal |
|----------|---------------------|----------------|-------------|
| Cold (TOF) | 1.5–2.5 | > 3.0 | > 4.0 |
| Warm (MOF) | 2.5–4.0 | > 5.0 | > 7.0 |
| Hot (BOF / Retargeting) | 4.0–8.0 | > 10 | > 15 |

*Hot audiences can tolerate higher frequency because they have high intent. Cold audiences fatigue quickly.*

## Attribution Notes

**Platform-reported numbers are always inflated.** Every platform claims credit for conversions.

| Platform | Over-reporting tendency |
|----------|------------------------|
| Meta | 20–60% over Google Analytics |
| Google Ads | 10–30% over GA4 |
| TikTok | 30–80% over attribution tools |

**Best practice:**
- Use 1-day click attribution window for most accurate signal
- Always compare platform data to GA4 or backend data
- Look at blended CAC (total ad spend / total new customers) as the north star metric
- UTM parameters are required for any cross-platform analysis
